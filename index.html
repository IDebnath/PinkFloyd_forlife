Description

Pink Floyd is an English rock band formed in 1965. They gained fame as one of the first British psychedelic groups. Pink Floyd was known for their extended compositions, light shows, and philosophical lyrics. The band was founded by students. By 2013, they became one of the best selling artists by selling 250 million records worldwide. Thoughout their music career, they have earned glamorous awards like the BAFTA award, the Grammy and the Polar Music Award.

Biography

The earlier Pink Floyd in 1965 had five members. They are Syd Barrett (lead and rhythm guitars, vocals), Nick Mason (drums percussions and vocals), Roger Waters (bass, vocals and rhythm guitar), Richard Wright (keyboards, piano, organ and vocals) and Bob Klose (lead guitar). Later, when Barrett left the band in April, 1968, Pink Floyd hired David Gilmour (lead and rhythm guitars, vocals, bass and keyboards) to replace Syd Barrett.
